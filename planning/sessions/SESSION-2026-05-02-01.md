### Reverse-drift fixture rationale (Task 1.3)

| Drift direction | Row state | Folder-only count | Status-only count | Conjunctive count | Inbox |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Forward (canonical) | folder=pending, status=failed | counts (incorrect) | excludes (correct) | excludes (correct) | excludes |
